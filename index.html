Bio

Hi, my name is Andrew and I'm a software engineer with a history of working 5+ years in front end web development but i was yearning for more. I had a strong desire and made it a goal to learn how to build meaningful web applications utilizing modern technologies that users could interact with and receive value. Prior to web development, i spent a few years working in the energy efficiency space executing analysis and managing energy data facilitated by Excel.

After my revelation above, I decided it was time to invest in myself and advance to the next level by joining a software engineering program offered by General Assembly to meet my aforementioned goal.

At the end of January 2023, i successfully completed the program with multiple projects to show for it which you can find in the projects page.n in the web development field for a number of years, I decided to invest in myself and advance by learning modern technologies in software engineering. The vision I have for myself is to build a career by strengthening my software skills
